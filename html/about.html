Neziah D. Bernabe

Writer • Creator • Human

Tides & Tears is my personal archive — a place where I put words to the things that shaped me. Some entries are heavy, some are hopeful, and some are just honest.

I built this site to remind myself that healing is not linear, and that life keeps moving — like waves, like breath, like a heartbeat.

Purpose

To document growth, honor pain, and leave proof that I kept going.

What you’ll find here

Stories, reflections, lessons, and the quiet parts in between.

Current note

This space is still unfolding. New pieces will appear over time as I’m ready to share them.
